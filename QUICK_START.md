# 🎊 Quick Start Guide - Enhanced Iftar Invitation

## How to Interact with Your Invitation

### 1. 🪔 **Lantern Reveal**
- **Click** the lantern in the center OR **scroll down** to trigger the reveal
- You'll hear a bell chime 🔔
- The invitation smoothly appears with elegant curtains opening

### 2. ☽ **Crescent Moon (Top-Right)**
- **Click once**: See sparkles ✨ float around the moon
- **Click twice**: More sparkles appear
- **Click three times**: 🎁 Secret message appears!
  > *"Ramzan evenings are better with friends like you."*

### 3. ⏰ **Countdown Timer**
- Shows days, hours, minutes, and seconds until Iftar
- The lantern emoji 🪔 has a glowing animation
- Updates every second
- Located above the food selection

### 4. 🍲 **Select Your Favorite Foods**
- Click any food item to select/deselect
- Selected items highlight with:
  - Gold border
  - Brighter background
  - Gold checkmark badge ✓
- Choose multiple items - no limit!
- Items selected here are for reference (separate from RSVP form)

### 5. 📋 **Complete Your RSVP**
- Fill in your name
- Choose your attendance (Yes/Maybe/No)
- Select menu items from the form sections
- Add dietary restrictions if needed
- Click "Confirm RSVP"
- View your summary and copy to clipboard

## 💡 Tips

1. **Sound**: Make sure your volume is on to hear the bell chime!
2. **Colors**: The gold (#d4af37) represents the luxurious Ramadan theme
3. **Animations**: All animations are smooth - if they feel jerky, refresh the page
4. **Easter Egg**: Try clicking the moon 3 times quickly!
5. **Copy Summary**: After RSVP, click "Copy to Clipboard" to share your response

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Lantern doesn't appear
→ Refresh the page (F5)

### Curtains don't open smoothly
→ Clear browser cache and reload

### Bell sound doesn't play
→ Check if audio is muted in your browser

### Sparkles don't appear
→ Check if JavaScript is enabled

### Countdown shows wrong date
→ This is intentional (set to March 15, 2026)

### Food selection doesn't highlight
→ Try clicking directly on the food icon or label
